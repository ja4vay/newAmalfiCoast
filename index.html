Rich History, Flavor and Beauty...

This popular tourist destination begins in the south Sorrento Peninsula and stretches 37 miles. Many small towns make up the "Amalfi Coast," although the most popular are Positano, Amalfi, Ravello, and the island of Capri.

Amalfi, Positano and Ravello are ideal locations because these towns offer many hotel options, and are the easiest to get to by public transportation. Ravello is perched above Amalfi and Positano, which makes it ideal for travelers seeking a quieter location. Other notable towns include Atrani, Meiori and Sorrento.
